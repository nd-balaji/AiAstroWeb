## The Core Principles of a Business Muhurtham

When an astrologer sets a Muhurtham for a marriage, they look for Venus (love) and the 7th House. But when setting a Muhurtham for a business, the focus shifts entirely to wealth, scaling, and public reputation.

### 1. Select a Fixed Ascendant (Sthira Lagna)
The Ascendant (Lagna) at the time of the business launch dictates the foundation of the company. If you want a business that is stable, long-lasting, and resistant to market crashes, you should generally select a **Fixed Sign** as the rising Ascendant:
*   **Taurus (Vrishabha):** Excellent for wealth generation, banking, and luxury goods.
*   **Leo (Simha):** Excellent for establishing authority, leadership, and massive public visibility.
*   **Scorpio (Vrischika):** Good for research, mining, and intense transformative industries.
*   **Aquarius (Kumbha):** Perfect for tech startups, humanitarian efforts, and large networked communities.

*Note: Movable signs (like Aries or Libra) are only preferred if the business requires constant travel or rapid, short-term turnover.*

### 2. Strengthen the Houses of Commerce
At the exact minute of the launch, the astrological chart should ideally have strong planets located in:
*   **The 10th House:** The house of career and public reputation.
*   **The 11th House:** The house of massive gains, profits, and networking.
*   **The 2nd House:** The house of accumulated bank balance.

### 3. Choose a Commercial Nakshatra (Star)
Not all stars are meant for business. You need Nakshatras that promote growth, movement, and wealth. The most highly recommended Nakshatras for commercial ventures include:
*   **Pushya:** The ultimate star for nourishment and wealth (especially good on a Thursday).
*   **Anuradha:** The star of success and networking.
*   **Ashwini:** Excellent for rapid beginnings and tech launches.
*   **Revati:** Brings wealth and ensures a smooth journey for the business.
*   **Rohini:** Promotes steady, beautiful, and massive, grounded growth.

## What to Strictly Avoid

A good Muhurtham is as much about avoiding negative cosmic energy as it is about capturing positive energy. Never launch a business during:
1.  **Rikta Tithis (Empty Lunar Days):** The 4th (Chaturthi), 9th (Navami), and 14th (Chaturdashi) lunar days are considered "empty hands" and are strictly avoided for wealth generation.
2.  **Rahu Kalam & Yamagandam:** The daily poisonous periods of the day based on your local sunrise.
3.  **New Moon (Amavasya) or Eclipses:** The Moon represents the mind and public reception. You do not want the public's perception of your brand to be hidden or eclipsed.
4.  **When the Moon is in the 8th House:** Make sure that the Moon at the time of the launch is not sitting in the 8th house from your personal birth Rasi (Chandrashtama).
